EXCO TECHNOPRENEUR AND GRADUATE EMPLOYABILITY

MPP UTHM 2022/2023

About

EXCO Technopreneur and Graduate Employability typically involve fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among students, as well as enhancing their employability skills. The EXCO Technopreneur is responsible for promoting and supporting a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship within the university community. The Graduate Employability focuses on preparing students for successful employment and careers after graduation. The EXCO Technopreneur and Graduate Employability roles complement each other by promoting innovation, entrepreneurship, and employability skills, thereby equipping students with the necessary tools to thrive in their careers and make valuable contributions to society.

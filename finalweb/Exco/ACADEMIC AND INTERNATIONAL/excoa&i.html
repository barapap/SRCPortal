About

Exco Academic

  • Responsible for monitoring activities related to academic UTHM students.
  • To provide activities that can improve academic achievement for UTHM students. The proposed activities such as organising seminars, forums and so on.
  • Apart from conducting motivational and academic programmes, it also aims to oversee the interests and welfare of students in the field of education.
  • Prepare reports for activities on a monthly and annual basis.

Exco International

  • Responsible for fostering the spirit of racial unity among UTHM students.
  • Detect and reduce social tension through a joint approach of UTHM students.
  • Responsible for the welfare of international students and carrying out activities related to international affairs in the context of universities and countries.
  • Provide monthly and annual activity reports.

We are thrilled to announce that MPP UTHM recently participated in the National Students Advocacy Workshop (NaSA) on the 10th of June 2023 (Saturday) organised by the Higher Education Malaysia Association, HEYA, in collaboration with MPP UTM.
This workshop aims to empower students and youth leaders by equipping them with the pivotal organizational knowledge and management skills to accelerate the formation of independent and effective student unions.
Moreover, the workshop focused on student union reform consultation, enabling us to provide valuable input and feedback to the Ministry of Higher Education for the upcoming AUKU reform.
We are excited about the knowledge gained, the connections made, and the opportunity to advocate for positive change within our educational system. Stay tuned for further updates as we continue our commitment to student empowerment!

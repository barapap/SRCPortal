EXCO WELFARE

MPP UTHM 2022/2023

About

The Exco Welfare role is to ensure the welfare of students in the university. This includes providing support for students who are struggling academically,financially, or emotionally. The Exco Welfare officer may also be responsible for organizing social events and activities for students.

UTHM SRC PORTAL

STUDENT UNION, ONE FOR ALL

The SRC serves as the voice of the students at UTHM, working tirelessly to represent your interests, address concerns, and enhance your university experience. Our mission is to foster a vibrant and inclusive campus community, where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Through this all-in-one portal, we aim to provide you with easy access to essential information, announcements, and resources. Stay up to date with the latest news from the SRC, browse through club activities, and find avenues to voice your opinions or submit complaints. We believe in transparency and accountability, and this portal serves as a central hub to bridge the gap between the SRC and the student body.

ABOUT US

A Distinct Identity

The Student Representative Council (SRC) is a vital student-led organization at UTHM that represents the collective voice of the student body. Committed to serving your needs and advocating for your rights, the SRC plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between students and the university administration.


As the official representative body, the SRC works tirelessly to address student concerns, provide support, and create a conducive environment for academic and personal growth. Our dedicated team of elected student leaders serves as the liaison between you and the university, ensuring that your opinions, ideas, and grievances are heard and acted upon.

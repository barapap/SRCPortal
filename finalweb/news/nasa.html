[𝐍𝐀𝐓𝐈𝐎𝐍𝐀𝐋 𝐒𝐓𝐔𝐃𝐄𝐍𝐓𝐒 𝐀𝐃𝐕𝐎𝐂𝐀𝐂𝐘 𝐖𝐎𝐑𝐊𝐒𝐇𝐎𝐏 (𝐍𝐚𝐒𝐀) x 𝐔𝐍𝐈𝐕𝐄𝐑𝐒𝐈𝐓𝐈 𝐓𝐄𝐊𝐍𝐎𝐋𝐎𝐆𝐈 𝐌𝐀𝐋𝐀𝐘𝐒𝐈𝐀] Assalamualaikum wbt and good day. We are thrilled to announce that MPP UTHM recently participated in the National Students Advocacy Workshop (NaSA) on the 10th of June 2023 (Saturday) organised by the Higher Education Malaysia Association, HEYA, in collaboration with MPP UTM. This workshop aims to empower students and youth leaders by equipping them with the pivotal organizational knowledge and management skills to accelerate the formation of independent and effective student unions. Moreover, the workshop focused on student union reform consultation, enabling us to provide valuable input and feedback to the Ministry of Higher Education for the upcoming AUKU reform. We are excited about the knowledge gained, the connections made, and the opportunity to advocate for positive change within our educational system. Stay tuned for further updates as we continue our commitment to student empowerment! Let's embrace this transformative experience and create a brighter future for all students! 🚀✨ That's all, thank you.

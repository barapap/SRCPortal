MAJLIS SAMBUTAN HARI RAYA AIDILFITRI

(KAMPUS CAWANGAN PAGOH)





âœ¨MAJLIS SAMBUTAN SETULUS KASIH HARI RAYA AIDILFITRI KAMPUS CAWANGAN, PAGOHâœ¨ğŸŒ™ Pada 15 Mei 2023, Majlis Perwakilan Pelajar dan Pejabat Hal Ehwal Pelajar telah menganjurkan Sambutan Setulus Kasih Hari Raya Aidilfitri bersama pelajar-pelajar di kampus cawangan Pagoh. Majlis ini juga turut dihadiri oleh Naib Canselor, Prof. Ir. Ts. Dr. Ruzairi bin Abdul Rahim, Timbalan Naib Canselor (Hal Ehwal Pelajar dan Alumni), Prof. Sr. Ts. Dr. Hj. Lokman Hakim Hj. Sedikit sebanyak kata-kata daripada pelajar-pelajar UTHM yang berbilang kaum menyatakan bahawa majlis ini mampu mengeratkan silaturahim di kalangan keluarga besar Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM). Moga ukhuwah ini terus berkekalan. Selamat Hari Raya Aidilfitri, Maaf Zahir dan Batin. On May 15, the UTHM Student Representative Council and the Student Affairs Office (PHEP) organized Eid al-Fitr Celebration with the theme of "Setulus Kasih" which means sincere care and love, with the students at the branch campus in Pagoh. The event was also attended by the Vice Chancellor, Prof. Ir. Ts. Dr. Ruzairi bin Abdul Rahim, Deputy Vice Chancellor (Student and Alumni Affairs), Prof. Sr. Ts. Dr. Hj. Lokman Hakim Hj. Ismail, University Executives, Heads of several Responsibility Centers (PTj) under HEPA, official representative the President of the Student Representative Council (MPP), the UTHM Student Leaders, and the all UTHM students. The students from diverse backgrounds expressed their views that this event has the potential to strengthen the bond among the Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) big family. May this unity among the UTHM community preserve and last. Wishing all a blessed Eid al-Fitr, Maaf Zahir dan Batin.
